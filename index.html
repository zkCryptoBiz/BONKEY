HOW TO BUY
Create a Wallet

Go to Settings in your wallet and locate Networks. You might have to manually add a network and input the following details for Base Chain:

Network Name: Base
RPC Endpoint: https://mainnet.base.org/
Chain ID: 8453
Currency Symbol: ETH
Block Explorer: https://basescan.org

Click on CONNECT TO BASE after saving. Upon completing this step, you should be able to connect to Base by choosing it from the network selection menu in your wallet.

Fund Your Wallet

To fund your wallet with Base ETH, you have some options:

  1. Transfer ETH from Coinbase, Binance, or whatever exchange you prefer to your Base wallet. Make sure you select Base as the network.
  2. You can utilize the following bridges to transfer ETH to Base ETH from another chain:
  • Base Bridge: https://bridge.base.org/deposit
  • Stargate Finance:https://stargate.finance/
  • Orbiter Finance: https://www.orbiter.finance/

To use one of these bridges, you must connect your wallet, choose the network you wish to bridge from, designate Base as the target network, and specify the amount of ETH you want to transfer.

CONNECT YOUR WALLET

1. Change the network in your wallet to Base.
2. Go to Uniswap at https://app.uniswap.org/explore/tokens/base/0x3C5fdF0ee37D62C774025599E3b692D027746E24
3. Connect your wallet with Uniswap.
4. In the lower swap field, choose a token and enter the official BONKEY on Base contract addreess, which is 0x3C5fdF0ee37D62C774025599E3b692D027746E24

Swap Your Tokens
  1. Input the amount of ETH you want to spend.
  2. Adjust the slippage to your preference by clicking on settings.
  3. Click or Tap Swap.
  4. Confirm in your wallet.
  5. You are done!
